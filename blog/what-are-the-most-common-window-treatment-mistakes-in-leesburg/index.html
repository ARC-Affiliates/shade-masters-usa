Window treatment myths vs facts

Myth: All plantation shutters are the same.

Fact: Materials range from basswood to polymer composites with vastly different humidity tolerances and 10-25 year lifespan differences.

Myth: DIY measurement saves money.

Fact: Roughly 1 in 3 DIY orders require costly remakes — professional measurement is typically free.

Myth: Honeycomb shades don't work in hot climates.

Fact: Cellular construction reduces heat gain by up to 60% per DOE data, making them ideal for Florida.

Myth: Cheaper window treatments perform similarly to premium ones.

Fact: Premium products typically last 2-3x longer and carry 15-25 year warranties versus 1-3 years.
