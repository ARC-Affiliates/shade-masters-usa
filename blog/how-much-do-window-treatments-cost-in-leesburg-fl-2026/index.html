How does the typical window treatment installation process work in 2026?

The installation process is the sequence from consultation through final inspection that converts measurements into installed products.

A standard window treatment project in Leesburg takes 2–6 weeks from consultation to final installation, depending on product customization and manufacturer lead time.

  1. Step 1: In-home consultation. A specialist evaluates window dimensions, sun exposure, and design goals — typically 45–90 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Precise measurement. Each opening is measured to 1/8-inch tolerance for inside or outside mount.
  3. Step 3: Quote and product selection. Homeowner receives an itemized estimate and selects fabrics, finishes, and operating systems.
  4. Step 4: Manufacturing. Custom orders ship from manufacturer in 10–25 business days.
  5. Step 5: Professional installation. Crew installs and levels treatments — typically 15–45 minutes per window.
  6. Step 6: Final walkthrough and warranty registration. Operation is demonstrated and warranty paperwork submitted to the manufacturer.

Who regulates window treatment safety and contracting in Florida?

Regulation comes from federal product-safety bodies and state business-registration agencies.

Window treatment products and installers in Florida are regulated by the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission, the Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation, and the Florida Department of State.

The CPSC enforces ANSI/WCMA A100.1-2018 cordless window covering standards nationwide (source: U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission). Florida Statute 489 governs construction contracting and Florida Statute 501 covers consumer-protection rules for home improvement contracts — including the 3-day right of rescission for in-home sales over $25 (source: Florida Senate, Chapter 501). According to Shade Masters USA, every contract issued in 2026 includes the mandated rescission notice and a written warranty. Homeowners near downtown Leesburg, Mount Dora, or Tavares can verify any installer's business standing through Sunbiz.org before signing.

#Window treatment myths vs. facts

Myth: All window treatments cost about the same.

Myth: Plantation shutters are too expensive to pay back.
